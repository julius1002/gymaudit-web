Precise workout documentation

GymAudit is a free german web application made for people who want to level up their sports experience and enjoy simplicity. It was designed to be used as a diary for workouts while keeping the idea of cross-device usage in mind.

More Features are on the way

GymAudit is currently under development by Julius Platon and this release is a first Demo version. More functionality as well as Internationalization is planned and will arrive through continuous updates.
